Boston Sports

Growing up in the Greater Boston area, professional sports have been in my life since the day I was born. Over the course of my life, I have gone to dozens of Patriots, Red Sox, Celtics and Bruins games. There aren't very many places in the world that bring me joy like Fenway Park, Gillette Stadium and TD Garden. Boston is undoubtedly the best sports city in the country, and that is what makes me lucky, and humbled, to live here.

Fenway Park: Home of the Boston Red Sox

Gillette Stadium: Home of the New England Patriots

TD Garden: Home of the Boston Celtics

TD Garden: Home of the Boston Bruins
